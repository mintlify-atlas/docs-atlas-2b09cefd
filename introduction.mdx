## What is OpenSight?

OpenSight is a comprehensive AI brand monitoring platform that helps you track how your brand appears across AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. In a world where AI is becoming the new front door to information discovery, understanding your AI visibility is critical.

## Why OpenSight?

Traditional SEO metrics don't tell the full story anymore. As AI-powered search becomes the primary way people discover information, you need visibility into:

- **How often AI engines mention your brand** when users ask relevant questions
- **What recommendations AI makes** about your products or services
- **Where competitors outrank you** in AI responses
- **Sentiment analysis** of how your brand is portrayed
- **Trending topics** in your industry that AI engines prioritize

<Note>
  OpenSight is open-source and can be self-hosted, giving you complete control over your brand monitoring data.
</Note>

## Key Features

### Multi-Engine Coverage

Monitor your brand across three major AI platforms:
- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI's GPT-4 and beyond)
- **Perplexity AI** (Real-time search with citations)
- **Google AI Overviews** (Google's AI-powered summaries)

### Automated Prompt Analysis

Define custom prompts relevant to your industry and automatically track how AI engines respond. OpenSight checks these prompts on a schedule and alerts you to changes.

### Competitor Benchmarking

Add up to 5 competitors per brand and see side-by-side comparisons of:
- Mention frequency
- Sentiment scores
- Position in AI responses
- Feature comparisons

### REST API Access

Integrate AI visibility data into your existing workflows with our comprehensive REST API:

```bash
curl -X GET "https://api.opensight.dev/brands" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
```

<Info>
  API access is available on all plans, including the free tier.
</Info>

## Tech Stack

OpenSight is built with modern, production-ready technologies:

- **Frontend**: Next.js 14 with React 18 and TypeScript
- **Backend**: Express.js with TypeScript
- **Database**: PostgreSQL 16 for reliable data storage
- **Cache**: Redis 7 for high-performance operations
- **Build System**: Turborepo for efficient monorepo management
- **Deployment**: Docker containerization for easy self-hosting

## Use Cases

### SaaS Companies

Track how your product is recommended by AI engines compared to competitors. Monitor sentiment around feature updates and identify gaps in AI visibility.

### E-commerce Brands

Understand how AI describes your products when customers ask for recommendations. Track competitive positioning and optimize product descriptions for AI discoverability.

### Marketing Agencies

Provide clients with comprehensive AI visibility reports. Monitor multiple brands and demonstrate the impact of content optimization efforts.

### Enterprise Teams

Self-host OpenSight for complete data control. Integrate with existing analytics tools via API and webhook notifications.
